Lasagna

Description

A vegetarian lasagna made with with shiitake mushrooms, spinach, ricotta, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • Shiitake mushrooms
  • Spinach
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Boil salted water for the pasta
  2. Prep mushrooms and cheese
  3. Saute the mushrooms
  4. Add onions
  5. Make the sauce
  6. Preheat oven and prep the oven dish
  7. Boil lasagna sheets
  8. Assemble lasagna
  9. Cover with foil and bake
