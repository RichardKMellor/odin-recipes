Veggie Shepherd's Pie

Description

A vegetarian shepherd's pie with with lentils, peas, mashed potato, carrots, and cheese on top!

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Peas
  • Mashed potato
  • Carrots
  • Grated Cheese
  • Tomato Paste
  • >Onions

Steps

  1. Boil salted water for the mashed potatoes
  2. Boil lentils
  3. Saute Vegetables
  4. Add onion
  5. Make the sauce
  6. Preheat oven and prep the oven dish
  7. Assemble pie in oven tray
  8. Add mashed potato and cheese on top
  9. Cover with foil and bake
